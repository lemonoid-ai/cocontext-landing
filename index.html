Lemonoid works for any hospitality or service business that receives online reviews. This includes restaurants, cafes, bars, hotels, spas, salons, and similar businesses. Our AI analyzes customer feedback patterns across all major review platforms (Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, etc.) to identify revenue-boosting opportunities specific to your industry and local market.

No more spending hours reading through reviews - our AI does the analysis and gives you only the insights that can drive more revenue.
